Nishaat Williams

Throughout my life art has been a calling. I first explored this calling with photography, doing freelance work as a wedding and baby photographer. After matriculating I foolishly decided that architecture is the only viable career with artistic aspects worth pursuing.

Not long after, I discovered my love for animation and am now in my second year at The Animation School. Not only have I delved into the art of animation, I have also explored and became engrossed in the digital art medium. I now do freelance work as a digital artist while I complete my studies.
